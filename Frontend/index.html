About Ministry of AYUSH

The Ministry of AYUSH develops, promotes, and disseminates traditional and alternative medicine systems in India. It plays a vital role in holistic healthcare and preventive medicine.

MedSetu portal integrates traditional AYUSH knowledge with modern diagnostic tools, making health management efficient for doctors, patients, and administrators.
